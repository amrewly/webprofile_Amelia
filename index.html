About Me

Greetings! My name is Amelia Ismania, born on August 1, 2002, in Sragen, Central Java. I have a profound passion for reading, writing, and drawing. When it comes to food, my absolute favorite is noodles, while my go-to drinks are chocolate milk and anything with a hint of chocolate flavor. I have a soft spot for chocolate ice cream as well.
Currently, I reside in Pekanbaru, where I embarked on my educational journey. I started my education at a local kindergarten in Pekanbaru and continued my elementary schooling in this vibrant city. As I progressed to middle school and high school, I decided to challenge myself by attending a boarding school located in West Sumatra.
My pursuit of knowledge led me to Muhammadiyah University in Riau, where I am currently in my fifth semester. It's been an incredible journey thus far, and I'm excited to see where it leads next.
I'm deeply passionate about personal growth and creativity, always seeking new experiences and challenges. My diverse educational background has allowed me to learn, adapt, and thrive in various environments. I look forward to the next chapters of my life's adventure and the opportunities to learn, create, and make a positive impact.
Thank you for taking the time to get to know me. Feel free to reach out or connect with me as I continue on this remarkable journey.
